Small Bespoke Furniture


Zoran has been making bespoke wooden furniture for many years. His love and admiration for wood, its texture and colour can clearly be seen in his work.

When required Zoran makes small decorative bespoke pieces of furniture for our clients in solid wood, MDF or a combination of materials.

Whether you require a bespoke size or an adaptation of an existing design, or perhaps the realization of your own design ideas, he will work with you on the design, offering a CAD plan and 3-D model of the piece, as well as sourcing the matched timbers which guarantee a designer look, complementing the room style and soft furnishings.

These are just a small sample of the pieces that Zoran has been asked to make by my client's or clients of interior designers that I work with. Most of these pieces are original designs involving the client and Zoran. The beauty of the pieces can clearly be seen in the simplicity of the design and the quality of their execution.
