Initial consultation is free!
The first consultation is free of charge for local clients. Later consultations may be charged depending upon travel time and expenses incurred.

The purpose of the initial consultation is to:
-Survey existing soft furnishings, decoration and furniture, colour, shape and size of rooms, and take measurements required for the estimates
-Establish customer preferences in style colour schemas
-Exchange ideas about type, style and colour of curtains, blinds or other room decoration (pelmets, cushions, throws…)
-Establish budget level for fabrics and making
-Discuss delivery dates

Based on the initial consultation we will produce estimates for the making and fabric quantities.

Next phases will follow after the customer approval.

Please bear in mind that the cost can go up or down depending on a final choice of design, fabrics, patterns, lining… All changes are always communicated to and approved by customers.

Curtain fabrics for any budget!
We can supply you fabrics from almost any range at retail prices, and often with some discount: Romo, Harlequin, Blendworth, Casamance, Nobilis, Jane Churchill, Osborne & Little, Designers Guild...

We also maintain a substantial range of samples available at my workshop to choose from or to help you in decisions on colour schemes, while sample books from other ranges can be provided on demand.

Of course, you are very welcome to supply your own fabrics for curtains, roman blinds or cushions.

Quality curtain poles, tracks and blind mechanisms!
In most my jobs we use curtain poles or curtain tracks from Byron & Byron, Florentina, Tiara, Barnwood, Tillys, Silent Gliss...

As with fabrics, we can supply curtain poles and tracks and blinds mechanisms for you and save you some time and hassle or you can chose your own supplier.

All our work delivered and fitted!
We normally fit all of our own work, and this cost is included in the quotation we supply. That includes the fitting of curtain poles and tracks, roman blinds, pelmets and curtain hanging and dressing. Skilful clients can make some savings by doing the fitting themselves.

For local clients delivery is usually free. Additional delivery charges may be incurred for oversized items (e.g. extremely large pelmet requiring a big van or a lorry).
