Blinds Making


Blinds are a practical, neat and elegant solution for window treatments. Roman blinds can make a statement with strong colours and bold patterns or in plainer, more neutral fabrics, they can add texture and offer simplicity to a more modern scheme. More traditional schemes can be enhanced with Austrian blinds to make a richer more opulent statement. Roller blinds offer quick mechanisms and minimal fuss.

Made to measure blinds


All of my blinds are bespoke, made to measure, and hand finished. The blinds can be lined (in sateen cotton lining, blackout or thermal), interlined or made out of sheer material. They can be fitted on the outside or in the inside recess of the window to show the window architrave. They can also be combined with pelmets and curtains to give a more layered and tailored look when required.
